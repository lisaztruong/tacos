What we do

Learning By Doing with Lux Science


As opposed to traditional science education, which can be passive and theoretical, our learner-centered program
immerses students in a plot-driven and task-based scientific experience. Lux Science offers something for all
types of learners with hands-on science kits and a 3D virtual environment that allows for self-directed discovery.
